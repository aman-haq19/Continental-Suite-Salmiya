Directions from Kuwait International Airport (KWI)

expand_more

The Continental Suite Salmiya is approximately a 20-minute drive from Kuwait International Airport (KWI). Airport limousine and registered taxi services are readily available upon arrival. For direct navigation, please use our address provided above. We recommend ride-sharing apps for convenient travel.

Public Transportation Options

expand_more

Salmiya is well-connected by public bus routes and ride-sharing services such as Uber and Careem. These provide an affordable and efficient way to explore the city and its surroundings from the convenience of our prime location. Our concierge can assist with booking private transport.
